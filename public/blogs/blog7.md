# Animations in React Native: A Practical Guide

Animation choices impact both performance and feel. Pick the tool that matches your UI and constraints.

## Quick Guide

- `LayoutAnimation` for simple layout transitions
- `Animated` for basic interactions
- `Reanimated` for complex, high-performance motion

## Performance Tips

Prefer transforms and opacity. Avoid layout-heavy animations when possible.

## Final Take

Make motion purposeful, smooth, and lightweight.
